Full Street Closure

The obstruction of a public street (curb to curb or EOP to EOP) such that no access is provided to the existing street for general vehicular traffic.

Lane Obstruction

The obstruction along on-street parking or street travel lane such that limited access is provided to the existing street for general vehicular traffic.

  • Major Striped

    - Any NCDOT street in Raleigh’s Jurisdiction with a marked centerline - Any street that is bound by the Hillsborough Street and Downtown Business District (see map) - Any City of Raleigh maintained street that is classified as a Mixed-Use or Major Street per the Unified Development Ordinance with a marked centerline

  • Minor Striped

    - Any City of Raleigh maintained street that is classified as a Sensitive Area or Local Street per the Unified Development Ordinance with a marked centerline

  • Major Non-Striped

    - Any NCDOT street in Raleigh’s Jurisdiction without a marked centerline - Any street that is bound by the Hillsborough Street and Downtown Business District without a marked centerline

  • Minor Non-Striped

    - Any City of Raleigh maintained street that is classified as a Sensitive Area or Local Street per the Unified Development Ordinance without a marked centerline
Sidewalk Obstruction

The obstruction of any sidewalk to allow pedestrian detouring and providing an accessible path for all pedestrians.

  • Sidewalk – Full Closure:

    - The obstruction of a sidewalk such that no access is provided to the existing sidewalk for general pedestrian traffic

  • Sidewalk – Auxiliary (AUX):

    - The obstruction of a sidewalk such that no access is provided to the existing sidewalk for general pedestrian traffic, but supplementary or alternative means such as cargo containers, scaffolding and temporarily constructed boardwalks/paths are provided to facilitate continued pedestrian movement through or adjacent to the obstructed sidewalk

  • Sidewalk - Partial Closure:

    - The obstruction of a sidewalk such that limited, ADA- and PROWAG-compliant access is provided to the existing sidewalk utilizing means such as barricades and traffic cones

  • Sidewalk Major:

    - Sidewalk along any NCDOT maintained street with a marked centerline - Sidewalk that is part of a Mixed-Use Street or Major Street along City of Raleigh maintained streets with a marked centerline per the Unified Development Ordinance - Sidewalk bound by the Hillsborough Street and Downtown Business District (see map)

  • Sidewalk Minor:

    - Sidewalk along any NCDOT maintained street without a marked centerline - Sidewalk that is part of a Sensitive or Local Street or along City of Raleigh maintained streets with a marked centerline per the Unified Development Ordinance

  • Miscellaneous Major Dumpster/POD:

    - Any Dumpster or POD placed on a street per Major Definition - If the area encompasses paid parking, the daily ParkLink fee plus the daily right-of-way fee applies

  • Miscellaneous Minor Dumpster/POD:

    - Any Dumpster or POD placed on a street per Minor Definition - If the area encompasses paid parking, the daily ParkLink fee plus the daily right-of-way fee applies
